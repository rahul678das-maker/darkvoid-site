Darkvoid Privacy

Privacy matters because trust matters.

This page explains how Darkvoid handles information submitted through the website, particularly through the contact form. The site is designed to remain simple, serious, and respectful of visitor privacy.

Darkvoid values privacy, clarity, and responsible handling of communication.

Privacy Policy

How information is handled on this website.

This privacy page is intended to explain, in a clear and direct way, what information may be collected, why it may be collected, and how it is used.

1. Information you provide directly

If you use the Darkvoid contact form, you may provide information such as your name, email address, organisation, enquiry type, and message content.

This information is used solely for the purpose of reviewing and responding to your enquiry.

2. How that information is used

Information submitted through the website is used only in connection with legitimate communication relating to Darkvoid, including research discussion, project dialogue, collaboration, advisory enquiries, or general correspondence.

  • to review and respond to enquiries
  • to understand the nature of an enquiry
  • to maintain appropriate communication where relevant

3. Data sharing

Darkvoid does not sell, trade, or knowingly share personal information submitted through the website for marketing or unrelated third-party purposes.

Information may be processed through trusted service tools used to operate the contact form or website infrastructure, but only as necessary for the website to function.

4. Cookies and tracking

At present, Darkvoid is designed as a simple informational website and does not intentionally use advertising trackers or unnecessary marketing cookies.

If this changes in the future, this page may be updated accordingly.

5. Data retention

Enquiry information may be retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to review, manage, and respond to communications, or to maintain a relevant record of correspondence where appropriate.

6. Your rights

If you have submitted personal information through this site and would like to request correction or deletion of that information, you may contact Darkvoid directly using the contact email provided on the website.

7. Contact regarding privacy

Questions relating to privacy or data handling may be directed to:

hello.darkvoidbyrd@gmail.com

8. Updates

This page may be updated from time to time if the website structure, tools, or communication processes change.
